# Prompt and Image Attribute Guide for Imagen on Vertex AI

## Prompt Writing Basics
- **Subject**: The object, person, animal, or scenery you want an image of.
- **Context**: The background or environment (e.g., outdoors, studio, city).
- **Style**: The artistic style (e.g., photorealistic, sketch, oil painting).

## Enhancing Quality
- Use descriptive adjectives and adverbs.
- Reference specific artists or art styles if applicable.
- **Image Quality Modifiers**:
    - General: "high-quality", "beautiful", "stylized"
    - Photos: "4K", "HDR", "Studio Photo", "professional photographer"
    - Art: "detailed", "by a professional"

## Text in Images
- **Keep it short**: Limit text to 25 characters or less.
- **Iterate**: Multiple attempts may be needed.
- **Multiple phrases**: Use 2-3 distinct phrases max.
- **Font style**: Specify general styles (e.g., bold, serif) rather than exact fonts.
- **Font size**: Use general terms like small, medium, large.
- Prompt example: `A poster with the text "Summerland" in bold font as a title...`

## Advanced Techniques
- **Photography Modifiers**:
    - Lighting: "natural", "dramatic", "golden hour"
    - Camera: "macro", "wide angle", "telephoto", "bokeh", "depth of field"
    - Film: "black and white", "polaroid"
- **Aspect Ratios**:
    - 16:9 (Widescreen) - Good for landscapes/banners.
    - 4:3, 1:1, etc.
- **Negative Prompts**: Describe what you *don't* want (e.g., "blur, distortion, bad text").

## Photorealistic Keywords
- **Portraits**: "35mm portrait", "film noir", "depth of field"
- **Still Life**: "Macro", "high detail", "studio lighting"
- **Landscape**: "Wide-angle", "long exposure", "sharp focus"
